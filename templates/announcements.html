ABOUT US

Velocity is a database-driven web application that helps the college transport department to more easily and efficiently manage their complete bus fleet. It eliminates most manual interventions and provides instant reports to the department on various metrics. These reports would have taken days in normal manual administrative methods.

Our web application, Velocity, is integrated into the college management ERP thus creating an integrated end-to-end management system. Velocity manages information such as vehicle numbers, driver allocation to the vehicle on a day-to-day basis. The payment module is also part of the app and offers parents different modes of online payment. It allows the college administration to revise bus fees and communicate it easily to all students and faculties.
